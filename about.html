Meet Adam

Hairdresser & owner · Spiky Hair Salon

Adam Ma is the hairdresser behind Spiky Hair Salon — a single-chair studio tucked into Glen Osmond. One chair, one hairdresser, and one client at a time: every appointment is unhurried, private, and entirely about you. No crowded salon floor, no rushing off to the next person.

Whether it's a sharp men's cut, a fresh restyle, a colour or a perm, Adam takes the time to listen first and get the details right. His clients keep coming back for the calm, careful attention — and the relaxing wash that rounds off every visit.

The space reflects the way he likes to work: light, fresh and quiet — a spot to switch off for an hour and leave feeling like yourself again. Most days you'll find him here in Glen Osmond; on Tuesdays he works from home in Lightsview by request. He speaks English, Cantonese and Mandarin, so you're welcome in whichever feels most comfortable.
